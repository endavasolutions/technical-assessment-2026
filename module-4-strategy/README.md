# Module 4: Strategic Technology Planning

## Objective
Demonstrate ability to align technology strategy with business objectives and communicate a compelling vision.

## Scenario
As the incoming CIO, you must present a 3-year technology strategy to the CEO and board of directors.

## Tasks

### Task 1: Technology Roadmap
Create a 3-year technology roadmap that addresses:
- Infrastructure modernization
- Security program development
- Digital transformation initiatives
- Talent and organizational structure

### Task 2: Budget Planning
Develop an annual technology budget including:
- Infrastructure and cloud services
- Software and licensing
- Security and compliance
- Personnel and training
- Contingency (15%)

### Task 3: Executive Presentation
Prepare a presentation that communicates:
- Current state assessment
- 3-year vision and strategy
- Key initiatives and milestones
- Budget requirements and ROI
- Risk considerations

## Deliverables
- 3-year technology roadmap
- Annual budget spreadsheet
- Executive presentation

## Evaluation Criteria
- Strategic thinking
- Business alignment
- Communication clarity
- Financial acumen
